My Favorite Story: Coraline

One of my favorite stories is Coraline by Neil Gaiman. It follows a young girl who discovers a hidden door in her new home that leads to a mirror version of her world. At first, everything seems perfect — her “Other Mother” is kind and attentive — but soon Coraline learns that this world is a trap. With courage and cleverness, she must rescue her parents and herself from being trapped forever.
